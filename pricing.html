Pricing

Most junk removal jobs are priced by volume (cubic yardage). Occasionally extra labor, access, and disposal needs can affect pricing as well. The ranges below are meant as general guidelines, since every junk removal job is a little different.

  • 1/8 Load$125
  • 1/4 Load$150
  • 3/8 Load$225
  • 1/2 Load$300
  • 5/8 Load$375
  • 3/4 Load$450
  • 7/8 Load$525
  • Full Load$600

What can change the price?

  • Volume of material
  • Labor and time required
  • Distance from the truck to the junk
  • Stairs, basements, attics, or tight access
  • Demolition or tear-out work
  • Special disposal requirements

Pricing is always confirmed before work starts.
